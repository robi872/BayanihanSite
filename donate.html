Donate

Donation guidelines help keep items safe, useful, and easy to distribute.

Recommended Donations

  • Rice, noodles, canned goods
  • Cooking oil, coffee, sugar
  • Hygiene items (soap, shampoo, toothpaste)
  • Clean bottled water (if needed)

Please Avoid

  • Expired items
  • Open packages
  • Damaged containers
