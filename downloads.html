Volunteer Checklist (Printable)

  • Checked the Schedule page for date/time
  • Wore clean comfortable clothing
  • Brought face mask / hair tie (if needed)
  • Followed safety and hygiene guidelines
  • Confirmed role: packing / kitchen / distribution / logistics

Donation Checklist (Printable)

  • Items are sealed and safe
  • Not expired, not opened, not damaged
  • Packed in a bag/box for cleanliness
  • Reviewed Donate page recommendations
  • Coordinated drop-off via Contact if needed
