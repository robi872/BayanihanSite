Safety Tips

Simple guidelines to keep food handling and distribution safe, respectful, and organized.

For Volunteers

  • Wash hands before packing and after handling items.
  • Use clean gloves when sorting cooked/ready-to-eat items.
  • Separate dry goods from wet/temperature-sensitive items.
  • Label bundles clearly to avoid confusion during distribution.
  • Follow coordinator instructions and assigned roles.

For Donors

  • Donate items that are sealed, safe, and not expired.
  • Avoid opened packs or damaged containers.
  • Pack items in a bag/box to keep them clean during transport.
  • If donating cooked food, coordinate timing to keep it safe.
  • Use the Donate page checklist before drop-off.

Respectful Distribution

  1. Maintain an organized queue and avoid pushing.
  2. Follow posted instructions and time slots if provided.
  3. Be kind—everyone is here to help the community.
